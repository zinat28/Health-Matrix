Myth vs Fact

Fact: Risk accumulates over time, but early prevention in younger adults significantly changes long-term outcomes.
Fact: Exercise and nutrition work together; high sodium and saturated fat can still worsen blood pressure and lipids.
Fact: Not all chest pain is cardiac, but pressure with breathlessness, sweating, or faintness needs immediate evaluation.
